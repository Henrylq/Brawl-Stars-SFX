# Brawl Stars SFX

A complete archive of sound effects, music, and voice clips from Supercell's Brawl Stars, organized by area of the game and pulled from the install-time asset pack of the most recent APK.

If you've ever wanted Shelly's shotgun blast and the voice line she calls out entering a match, the main menu theme, the tap of a Starr Drop opening, or the music that plays on the win screen — it's all in here, named the way the game names it and sorted so you can actually find it.

**Game version:** 68.250 (~190 MB on disk)

## Contents

7211 OGG Vorbis audio files across 8 top-level categories:

| Category | Files | Category | Files |
| -------- | ----: | -------- | ----: |
| Brawlers | 6218  | Shop     |  101  |
| Game     |  288  | Boxes    |   84  |
| Events   |  313  | Currency |   25  |
| Menu     |  175  | Robot    |    6  |

One standalone file — the Supercell jingle (`supercell_jingle.ogg`) — sits at the repository root.

- **Brawlers** — every brawler's combat and voice audio, one folder per brawler, with that brawler's skin sounds folded in alongside the base set: attacks, supers, gadgets, and reloads, plus the voice set (entering battle, taking damage, scoring a takedown, holding the lead, and being defeated). This is the overwhelming bulk of the archive.
- **Events** — season- and event-themed audio and jingles, grouped by event: Arcade, Brawl-o-ween, Brawlzilla, Football, Mecha, Royale, Starr Park, Winter, Dragons and Fairies, BXS, Stranger Things, Angels and Demons, Cyber Noodles, Katana Kingdom, Windstock, and All You Can Brawl.
- **Game** — generic gameplay audio that isn't tied to one brawler, with subfolders for Abilities, Modifiers, Music (in-game loops and the result/draw/win/lose stings), Tutorial, and Old.
- **Menu** — interface and navigation sounds, with Brawl Pass and Progress subfolders.
- **Shop** — shop and offer-reveal audio, including the Juju's Oddities Shop section: the shopkeeper's voice lines and reactions plus its background muzak.
- **Boxes** — box, Starr Drop, egg, vault, and loot-opening sounds.
- **Currency** — coin, gem, key, ticket, power-point, and trophy tally and pickup sounds.
- **Robot** — Robo Rumble.

## Format

Brawl Stars ships its audio as standalone OGG Vorbis files — unlike Clash Royale, there are no FMOD sound banks to unpack, so every clip here is an original game stream lifted straight from the asset pack with nothing re-encoded or modified. Most files are 44.1 kHz, with a small share at 48 kHz or 22.05 kHz, and they run mostly mono with a stereo minority. The streams are unmodified — the original audio as the game ships it, suitable as-is for use in audio editors (Audacity, Reaper, Ableton, FL Studio), DAWs, sample packs, ringtones, content-creation workflows, and reference work.

## Updates

Each refresh replaces the entire tree to match the latest game build. Future game updates are not guaranteed to land here.
